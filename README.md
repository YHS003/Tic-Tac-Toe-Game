#How to play?
1- For Playing Enter Number between 1 and 9
2- Each Number Acts a square in the board like this:

   1 | 2 | 3
  ---|---|---
   4 | 5 | 6
  ---|---|---
   7 | 8 | 9

  3- Player 1 is X and (Player 2)/(Computer) is O
